Key Takeaways

  • Inspect canopy overhang near roofs, garages, and utility drops.
  • Look for deadwood, fungal signs, and trunk cracks around mature trees.
  • Estimate near-term trimming and removal needs for budgeting.

Inspection priorities

Focus on trees that can affect structure, drainage, and access safety.

  • Roofline contact
  • Fence and driveway impact
  • Root conflict near hardscape

Budget planning

A pre-close tree estimate can help avoid surprise ownership costs.

  • First-year trim schedule
  • Hazard removal needs
  • Stump or debris backlog

Action Checklist

  • Document visible hazards
  • Request local estimate
  • Plan year-one tree budget

Guide FAQ

Should tree issues affect offers?

Significant hazards or deferred maintenance can influence negotiation strategy.

Can old trees always be saved?

Not always. Structural condition determines whether pruning or removal is safer.

When should inspection happen?

Before final close whenever possible.
